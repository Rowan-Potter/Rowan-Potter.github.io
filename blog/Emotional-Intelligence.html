Emotional Intelligence?

What is Emotional Intelligence?
Emotional Intelligence is your own ability to monitor your own emotions and physical feelings, EQ is to translate all the cryptic messages your body and brain are sending you and turn them into usefull information you can learn from.

How does EQ differ from IQ?
IQ can be increased but for the most part it is something your born with, it is a meassure of your natural crititcal thinking skils, like solving a maths equation.
EQ on the other hand is mostly learnt, EQ has to be worked for. EQ is a meassure of your natural emotional thinking skills,
like realising a sudden burst of anger is actually linked to your own anxietys or even picking up on your body not enjoying a food type you thought it did.

Why is EQ important?
EQ is important in every layer of life, from work life to home life to your friendships and families.
Having a high EQ will help you with self motivation it will help you overcome your own anxieties, it even helps you learn from your mistakes.
In conclusion having a high EQ will help you lead a balanced and healthy life.
